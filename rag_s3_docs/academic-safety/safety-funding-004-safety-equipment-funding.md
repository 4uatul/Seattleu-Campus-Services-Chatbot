## Content

Safety Equipment Funding program provides financial support for safety enhancements. Each academic department may request up to $350 to purchase reusable items addressing safety concerns. Full amount or cost sharing. Multiple requests per fiscal year (July 1-June 30). Priority to departments not previously funded. Complete Safety Equipment Funding Request form with item list and short safety explanation. Supply orders placed through ProcureSU. EXAMPLES: vented caps for chemical waste, machine guards, ergonomic equipment (biosafety cabinet arm rests), replacement burner tubing, reusable PPE (flame-resistant Nomex lab coats, laser safety glasses, thermoprotective gloves).
