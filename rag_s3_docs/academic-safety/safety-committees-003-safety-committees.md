## Content

Safety committees allow community members to share info and improve safety. UNIVERSITY SAFETY COMMITTEE: promotes safety/health, evaluates Accident Prevention Program, reviews incident/accident reports. Up to 5 members. INSTITUTIONAL BIOSAFETY COMMITTEE (IBC): oversees research/teaching with recombinant or synthetic nucleic acid molecules per NIH Guidelines. Faculty-led, includes unaffiliated community members. BUILDING SAFETY COMMITTEES: discuss safety topics specific to building—emergency procedures, incidents/near misses, building issues, notices. Current committees at Sinegal and Bannan. TOPIC-SPECIFIC: Lab Safety Committee, Shop Safety Committee.
