## Content

Online safety training through EngageSU. Student employees access modules on Student Employees training page. IN-PERSON/VIRTUAL LIVE: Biosafety Fundamentals (BSL1), Biosafety Level 2 (BSL2), Bloodborne Pathogens, Compressed Gases, Cryogens, Laboratory Safety Orientation, Risk Assessment. HANDS-ON: Fire Extinguisher Selection and Use, PPE Donning and Doffing, Preparing Samples for Transport, Train the Trainer, Working in a Biosafety Cabinet. CUSTOM TRAININGS: Academic Safety Officer can develop custom training—contact ASO.
