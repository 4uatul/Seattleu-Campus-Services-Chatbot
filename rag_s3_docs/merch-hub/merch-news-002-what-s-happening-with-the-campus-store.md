# What's Happening with the Campus Store

**Section:** Merch Hub
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > Campus Store > News

## Content

Tiered transition beginning late November with full launch of new Campus Store in 2025. Online Bookstore ready for course material orders November 15th. Physical Campus Store changes begin November 20th. In collaboration with Affordable Learning @ Seattle University, students get options for new/used books, course rentals, market pricing. NEW OPERATORS: ECampus.com manages course materials via online portal—easiest/fastest/most affordable way to buy/rent/sell textbooks and digital content. No physical store for last-minute book purchases; shop online with new/used/rental/digital options. Shipping to Campus Store free or personal address with charges. Event Network spearheads merchandise and experience—proven track record at museums/zoos/aquariums, focuses on local diverse makers, environmentally and socially conscious.
