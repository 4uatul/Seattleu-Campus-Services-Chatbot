## Content

Notary Services for students, faculty, staff, campus affiliates, and community by APPOINTMENT ONLY. Book through Supercopy Notary Bookings. For DEPARTMENT BILLING: Book appointment, forward email confirmation to repro-notaries@seattleu.edu with 17-digit accounting code (e.g., 11-0-4-123456-1234567), number of notary stamps needed, special requests. Appointments must be scheduled at least 2 hours in advance. Hours: Mon-Thur 8:30-11:30 AM and 1:30-3:30 PM; Fri 8:30-11:30 AM. No weekend appointments.
