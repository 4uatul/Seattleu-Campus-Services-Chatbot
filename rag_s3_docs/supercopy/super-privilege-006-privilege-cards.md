# Privilege Cards

## Content

Privilege Cards enable University campus affiliates (vendors, guests, Non-Student Sublessees) to access University privileges: door access, printing, copying, payment systems. Copy Cards are Privilege Cards. HOW TO GET: Look for Privilege Cards/Supplementary ID Badges form in ProcureSU. Single entry in form or Excel upload (Privilege-Cards-Template.xlsx). Must put in Service Desk request before submitting form to get ID numbers, door access, and Print Group additions.
