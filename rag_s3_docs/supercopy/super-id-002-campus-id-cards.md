# Campus ID Cards

**Section:** Supercopy
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > Supercopy > Campus Cards
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/CampusIDCards.aspx
**Tags:** ID card, Campus Card, photo, replacement, eAccounts

## Content

Campus Card is photo ID for SU students, faculty, staff. Used for: building access, library card, printer agent (24-hour first activation), gym pass, meal plans at Redhawk Dining. First-time cards FREE. REPLACEMENT: $20 plus tax for lost cards. Pay/pickup at Supercopy. TEMPORARY LOANER CARD: free for 7 days, must return. EACCOUNTS: Manage card at Transact eAccounts Portal—submit photo, review activity/balance, deposit money. Family can add money using SU ID number. LOCKED OUT: Contact HelpDesk 206-296-5571 or servicedesk@seattleu.edu. LOST/STOLEN: Login to eAccounts to deactivate, then notify Public Safety. After-hours: 206-296-5990. NEW STUDENTS: Freshmen/transfer can submit selfie via Transact eAccounts Portal (email from onlinephoto@transactcampus.com). Photo criteria: full face, neutral expression, no shadows, white/off-white background, no sunglasses, head coverings only for religious/medical. Resident students can pick up card at residence hall front desk during move-in. Upperclassmen/staff/faculty pay $20+tax to update photo. NO BUILDING ACCESS: Email keys2@seattleu.edu or Public Safety Portal. After-hours: 206-296-5990.
