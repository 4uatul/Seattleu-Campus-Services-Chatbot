## Content

PERISHABLE MAIL POLICY: Mailing Services does NOT provide refrigeration. Contact MailingServices@seattleu.edu in advance for perishable items. Not responsible for spoilage if no arrangement made. CARRIER SERVICES (official University mail only; personal mail at Supercopy): USPS pickup 1:30 PM (drop at Supercopy 30 min prior for same-day): First Class, Pre-sort, Priority, Certified/Return Receipt, Delivery Confirmation, Express, Media Mail, Library Rate, International. FedEx pickup 3:00 PM: First Overnight, Priority Overnight, Standard Overnight, 2Day, Express Saver, Ground, International Priority/Economy/First. UPS pickup 3:00 PM (pickup only). Customs forms completed online via USPS website, printed, signed, attached.
