## Content

BLEEDS & MARGINS: A bleed is image/color/artwork extending beyond trim line to ensure no white border after cutting. Most printers cannot print to edge—leaves ~1/4 inch white margin. With bleed, oversized paper is used and trimmed. Leave minimum 1/4 inch margin between text and trim line. COLOR SPACES: RGB (Red, Green, Blue) used by screens (3 lights combine to white). CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black) used for printing (4 inks combine to black). PDFs designed to translate RGB to CMYK. PNG uses RGB—not recommended for print. DPI/RESOLUTION: Screens need only 72 DPI; print needs 300 DPI minimum. For large format banners aim for 600 DPI. FILE FORMAT: PDF best. Also accepts DOC, DOCX, EPS, JPG, PSD, TIF. Not recommended: BMP, GIF, PNG, PPT, TXT. BRAND GUIDELINES: Marketing Communications (MarCom) has resources—logos, typefaces, templates, brand guidelines. MarCom manages Canva access.
