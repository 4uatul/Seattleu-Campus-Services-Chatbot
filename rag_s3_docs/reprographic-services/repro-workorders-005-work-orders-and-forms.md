## Content

Reprographic Services partners with Supercopy for course materials (copyright clearance, production, sale to students), prints SU letterhead/envelopes/business cards. Files preferred in PDF format. Save as PDF from Office applications or print to PDF from Windows. Also accept Office files and JPEG/TIFF graphics. Copy/print/bindery turnaround: 1-2 business days. Rush orders accommodated. BUSINESS CARDS for Faculty/Staff via Online Digital Storefront. Student business cards: Supercopy. OFFSET PRINTING (envelopes, letterhead): Online Digital Storefront, 5-10 business days turnaround. Rush available with additional fees. Located Garden Level (basement) of Student Center Pavilion (west side of Bellarmine Hall).
