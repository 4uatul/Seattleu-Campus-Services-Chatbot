## Content

Seattle University is non-profit and qualifies for discounted bulk mail rate. PLAN AHEAD: Bulk mail printing/prepping/delivery takes 4-10 business days. Once at post office, carrier-route-sorted bulk mail takes 2 business days for Seattle area, add 1 day per zone outside. BULK VS NON-PROFIT: Some SU functions considered profit-making (e.g., cruise event for club fundraising = Bulk Rate). Retreat with charge = Non-Profit rate. Check first. QUALIFICATIONS: 200+ addressed pieces eligible for discount (under 200 must be First Class). At least 150 pieces must share first 3 zip digits. Postcards: 3.5x5 to 4.25x6 inches. Letters: 3.5x5 inches/0.007 thick to 6.125x11.5 inches/0.25 thick. Flats: 6.125x11.5/0.25 thick to 12x15/0.75 thick. Max weight: Letters under 3.3 oz, Flats under 16 oz. Each piece marked Non-Profit Organization with correct permit number. BUSINESS REPLY MAIL: Template from MarCom or Reprographics. THIRD-PARTY: Obtain approval 10 business days before mail date. Submit ProcureSU requisition, get PO, fill out Bulk Mail Permit Preapproval Form. Postage charged plus $0.004/item.
