# Bulk Mail Third-Party

**Section:** Reprographic Services
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > Mailing > Bulk Mail 3rd Party
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Bulk-Mail-3rd-Party.aspx
**Tags:** bulk mail, third-party, permit, ProcureSU, vendor

## Content

Planning bulk mailings off-campus using University Bulk Mail permit requires 10 business days advance approval. Steps: 1) Submit ProcureSU requisition, 2) Await Purchase Order, 3) Fill out Bulk Mail Permit Preapproval Form. Include vendor email for direct confirmation. All bulk mailings charged postage + $0.004/item. Failure to obtain preauthorization or vendor errors with PO number may result in additional fees. Repeated issues may result in loss of access to bulk mail permit.
