## Content

Reprographic Services provides high-quality printing, copying, and bindery services. Services include postcards, brochures, large-format posters for SU students, staff, faculty, and most for general public. Course Packs handled by eCampus. Can assist with mailing orders through Mailing Services; provides Bulk Mail services. Staff/Faculty place orders online. Students/General Public review general copying and printing services. Orders take 2-3 business days (48-72 hours). RUSH FEE: $30 same-day. Exams and class materials exempt from rush fee. Digital files must be print-ready; proofread documents. Changes by Reprographic Services subject to $30/hour pro-rated service fee. FILE FORMAT: PDF preferred. Acceptable: DOC, DOCX, EPS, JPG, PDF, PSD, TIF. Not recommended: BMP, GIF, PNG, PPT, TXT. COLOR SPACE: CMYK (not RGB). RESOLUTION: 300 DPI minimum. PAGE BLEEDS: 0.125 inch (3mm) beyond document edge for edge-to-edge designs. Contact: Pavilion 020, 206-296-6180, reprographic@seattleu.edu. Hours: Mon-Thur 8:30 AM-4:00 PM, Fri 8:30 AM-2:00 PM, weekends/holidays closed.
