## Questions and Answers

### Q: How long do printing orders take at Reprographic Services?

A: Orders generally take 2-3 business days (48-72 hours). A $30 rush fee applies for same-day orders. Exams and class-related materials are exempt from the rush fee.

### Q: What file formats does Reprographic Services accept?

A: PDF preferred. Acceptable: DOC, DOCX, EPS, JPG, PDF, PSD, TIF. Not recommended: BMP, GIF, PNG, PPT, TXT. Use CMYK color space and 300 DPI minimum resolution.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/ReprographicServices.aspx

Tags: file format, specifications

### Q: What is a bleed in printing?

A: A bleed is image/color/design that extends 0.125 inch (3mm) beyond the document's final trim edge, ensuring no white border appears after the page is cut.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/TipsandTricks.aspx

Tags: bleed, design

---

### Q: What's the difference between RGB and CMYK?

A: RGB (Red, Green, Blue) is used by screens, combining 3 light hues. CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black) is used for printing, combining 4 inks. PDFs translate RGB to CMYK for accurate print.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/TipsandTricks.aspx

Tags: color, RGB, CMYK

### Q: How do I use the university's bulk mail permit with a third-party vendor?

A: Obtain approval 10 business days before mail date. 1) Submit ProcureSU requisition. 2) Await Purchase Order. 3) Fill out Bulk Mail Permit Preapproval Form. All bulk mailings charged postage + $0.004/item.
