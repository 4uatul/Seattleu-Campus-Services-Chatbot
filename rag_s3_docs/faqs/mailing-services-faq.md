## Questions and Answers

### Q: What do I do if my package is missing?

A: Submit a query to the Lost Package Form. Mailing Services will investigate whether it is delayed or lost.

### Q: Which residence halls does Mailing Services NOT deliver to?

A: Mailing Services does NOT deliver to Kolvenbach Community, Logan Court Townhomes, or Douglas Apartments. Each has its own mailing address.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/FrequentlyAskedQuestions.aspx

### Q: When is mail picked up from campus departments?

A: Mail picked up by noon (12:00 PM) Mon-Fri. USPS picks up from Mailing Services by 1:00 PM Mon-Fri.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/FrequentlyAskedQuestions.aspx

Tags: pickup, schedule

### Q: What are USPS, FedEx, and UPS pickup times?

A: USPS: 1:30 PM. FedEx: 3:00 PM. UPS: 3:00 PM (pickup only). Drop off packages at Supercopy 30 minutes prior for same-day service. Customs forms must be completed online via USPS website, printed, signed, attached.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/ShippingServices.aspx

Tags: carrier, shipping

---

### Q: Does Mailing Services accept cash payments?

A: No. Mailing Services processes mail for University departments only using budget numbers. For other payment methods, go to Supercopy.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/FrequentlyAskedQuestions.aspx

Tags: payment, cash

---

### Q: How should I handle perishable mail?

A: Mailing Services does NOT provide refrigeration. Contact MailingServices@seattleu.edu in advance to schedule delivery or arrange pickup. Not responsible for spoilage if no arrangement made.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/ShippingServices.aspx

Tags: perishable, delivery

### Q: Can I borrow a hand truck from Mailing Services?

A: Faculty and staff may borrow a hand truck only after noon (12:00 PM), day of. You must present a valid state-issued ID or another collateral item to be held during the loan.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/FrequentlyAskedQuestions.aspx

### Q: What's the minimum number of pieces for bulk mail discount?

A: 200+ addressed pieces eligible for discount. Under 200 must be First Class. At least 150 pieces must share first 3 digits of the zip code.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/BulkMail.aspx

Tags: bulk, qualification

---

### Q: How long does bulk mail processing take?

A: Printing, prepping, and delivery to USPS takes 4-10 business days. Once at post office, carrier-route-sorted bulk mail takes 2 business days for Seattle area, add 1 day per zone outside.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/BulkMail.aspx

Tags: bulk, timing
