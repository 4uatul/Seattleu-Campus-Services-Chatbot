## Questions and Answers

### Q: How do I buy course materials and textbooks?

A: Through the Online Bookstore at seattleu.ecampus.com. Options include new/used/rental/digital. Purchases can be shipped free to the Campus Store or to a personal address with shipping charges.

Source: https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Campus-Store.aspx
