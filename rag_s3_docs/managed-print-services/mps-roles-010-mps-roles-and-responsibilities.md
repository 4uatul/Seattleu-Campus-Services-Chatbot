# MPS Roles and Responsibilities

**Section:** Managed Print Services
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > MPS > Roles
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Roles-and-Responsibilities.aspx
**Tags:** MPS, roles, Copiers Northwest, ITS, responsibility

## Content

CAMPUS SERVICES: overall program management—accounting/billing, printer allocation, exception process. COPIERS NORTHWEST (CNW): hardware/software vendor—maintaining printers, delivering paper/toner, training (request through Service Desk). INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SERVICES (ITS): technical support—configuring workstations, deploying software, maintaining data lines and servers. USERS: submit service requests when issues arise, take surveys.
