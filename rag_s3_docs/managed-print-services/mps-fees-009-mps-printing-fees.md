# MPS Printing Fees

## Content

All charges per page of ink. BLACK & WHITE: First Hill $0.08 one-sided, $0.16 double-sided. SLU/Cornish $0.01 one-sided, $0.02 double-sided. COLOR: First Hill $0.18 one-sided, $0.36 double-sided. SLU/Cornish $0.25 one-sided, $0.50 double-sided. Prices for Letter (8.5x11) and Legal (8.5x14). Tabloid/Ledger (11x17) costs double.
