# MPS Program Goals and Successes

**Section:** Managed Print Services
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > MPS > Goals
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Program-Goals-and-Successes.aspx
**Tags:** MPS, goals, sustainability, successes

## Content

WHY MPS: Sustainability (robust repairable hardware, reduced environmental impact, less paper, more energy efficient, recycling toner cartridges). Ease of Use (more devices in public spaces, single print queue, consistent experience, reliability—every printer is your printer). Cost Savings (eliminate inkjet, reduce power/paper costs). Program Governance (single coordinated program, visibility through reporting, secure handling, unified service model). SUCCESSES since 2015: 70%+ reduction in printer density, 40% reduction in monthly printing volumes, eliminated need for staff inventory management, prints can be released from any location.
