## Content

South Lake Union (Cornish) campus operates on separate MPS configuration. MPS is unified campus-wide system delivering self-service printing, copying, scanning. Administers all print-capable devices except School of Law and small exceptions. Allows printing from any device, release from anywhere using standardized equipment maintained centrally. SU PRINT QUEUE: Send to su-print-queue, then tap/swipe card at any MPS machine to print. UNIVERSITY COMPUTERS: su-print-queue appears as printer (Ctrl+P). PERSONAL PRINTING: Download PaperCut for Mac/PC. GUEST PRINTING: Purchase preloaded guest card from Supercopy (Pavilion 010).
