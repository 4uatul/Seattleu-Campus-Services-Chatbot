# MPS Program Exceptions

**Section:** Managed Print Services
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > MPS > Exceptions
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Program-Exceptions.aspx
**Tags:** MPS, exceptions, third-party, vendors, policy

## Content

Purchase/reimbursement of printers and toner/ink RESTRICTED at FH and SLU. Needs not covered by program may be eligible for exception, require approval. Without approved exception: cannot use university funds, ProcureSU rejected, Pro Card rejected, reimbursements denied, ITS won't service/configure/network. REQUEST: fill out MPS Program Exception Request form. Exceptions for individual devices in specified location for approved business need. Not transferable. THIRD-PARTY VENDORS: must opt in to MPS or maintain own printers for internal business use. Vendors may NOT provide printing/copying to university affiliates outside MPS. Includes free/paid, self-service/assisted, temporary/pilot. All printer deployments reviewed in advance. Unauthorized deployments may be removed.
