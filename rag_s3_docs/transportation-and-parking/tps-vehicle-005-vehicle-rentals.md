# Vehicle Rentals

**Section:** Transportation & Parking
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > TPS > Vehicle Rentals
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/VehicleRentals.aspx

## Content

TPS provides 4 twelve-passenger vans and 1 ten-passenger wheelchair-accessible van. Beginning Winter Quarter 2026, biometric key box in Columbia Building atrium. All drivers must complete University Authorized Driver process. Questions: vanrental@seattleu.edu. CHARGES: 0-3 hours $35, Over 3 hours $70/day. Mileage $0.55/mile after first 100 miles. One-way over 250 miles requires second authorized driver. LATE/CLEANING: Late <1.5hrs $15, >1.5hrs $30. Dirt $10, trash $25, food $50. Smoking $50. Missing inspection forms $15 each. <1/2 tank gas $25. Lost gas card $50. EMS reservation opens: Spring 2026 Feb 24, Summer 2026 May 19, Fall 2026 Aug 18, Winter 2027 Dec 1.
