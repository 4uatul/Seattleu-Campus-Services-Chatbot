# Virtual Guest Parking

**Section:** Transportation & Parking
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > TPS > Guest Parking
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/DepartmentalGuestParking.aspx
**Tags:** guest, parking, virtual, permit, EVITE

## Content

Guest parking permits are VIRTUAL. Must be booked 3 business days in advance. Fill out Virtual Guest Parking Request with University Budget Line number for billing. Takes 1-3 business days to generate EVITE. Send EVITE link to your guest who enters their license plate. Same-day requests not guaranteed; call 206-296-5992 to check. RATES: 0-2 hrs $6, 2:01-4 hrs $10, Full Day $15, Evenings (after 5pm) $8, Sundays $8. Only for University guests, not for student/faculty/staff parking. Billed for total registered license plates.
