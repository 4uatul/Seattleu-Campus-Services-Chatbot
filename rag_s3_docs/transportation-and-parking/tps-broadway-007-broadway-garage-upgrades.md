## Content

Starting April 30, entry requires valid Seattle U Campus ID Card for both vehicle and pedestrian entrances. Mesh security fence installed around street level. You need ID to re-enter through locked pedestrian doors even if garage doors are open. Admittance does NOT confirm parking permit is valid. Garage monitored 24/7. Garage access issues: Public Safety 206-296-5990. Permit questions: Parking Services 206-296-5992.
