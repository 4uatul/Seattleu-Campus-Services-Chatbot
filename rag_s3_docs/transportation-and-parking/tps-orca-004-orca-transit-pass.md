## Content

Seattle University offers students and employees public transit options. STUDENTS: ORCA LIFT (reduced fare via King County Public Health, must qualify), ORCA Standard (50% subsidized through University at beginning of each term), ORCA Youth Card (students 18 and younger register via ORCA website). EMPLOYEES: Benefits-eligible employees can enroll in Employee ORCA program for subsidized Sound Transit Regional Transit Pass. Washington State Ferry passes available for additional monthly charge.
