## Content

Permits required all hours all days. Virtual permits require accurate license plate registration matching state registration. Incorrect plates can result in citations. URL: seattleu.thepermitstore.com. SU employees/students sign in with SU credentials. Best to use desktop/laptop with Chrome or Firefox. Tutorials cover: ordering permits, submitting appeals, registering vehicles, associating vehicles to permits, ordering Pay by the Day, finding expiration dates, viewing permits, finding receipts, removing items.
