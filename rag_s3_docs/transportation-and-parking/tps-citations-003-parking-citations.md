# Parking Citations

**Section:** Transportation & Parking
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > TPS > Citations
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Citations.aspx
**Tags:** citations, Impark, appeal, payment

## Content

Parking citations on First Hill and SLU campuses managed by Impark. PAY: Visit www.impark.com and click Pay a Notice. APPEAL: Visit payments.impark.com and click Open Messenger (red button, bottom-right). Seattle University TPS no longer processes citation payments or appeals directly.
