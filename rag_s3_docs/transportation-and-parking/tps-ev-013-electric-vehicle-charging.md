# Electric Vehicle Charging

**Section:** Transportation & Parking
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > TPS > EV Charging
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Electric-Vehicle-Charging.aspx
**Tags:** EV, electric vehicle, charging, parking

## Content

Seattle University has 10 EV chargers: 8 at north end of Pigott Visitor Lot (P1) and 2 in 13th & East Cherry Lot (P6). REGULATIONS: Must have valid SU parking permit activated. EV charger spots exempt from Pigott Visitor Lot permit restrictions for employees/students while charging. Maximum charging time 4 hours then move to spot allowed under permit. Cannot unplug another vehicle. Cannot charge while parked in non-EV-designated spot. Report faulty chargers/violations: ParkingServices@seattleu.edu.
