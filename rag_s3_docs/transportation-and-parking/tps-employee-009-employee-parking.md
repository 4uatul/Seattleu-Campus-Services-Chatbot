## Content

Faculty/Staff/Employee permits NOT valid in Pigott Visitor Lot (P1) 9am-3pm Monday-Friday. Permits: Faculty & Staff Annual (payroll deduction, valid until cancelled), Academic Year (payroll, renews each academic year), Carpool (payroll, renews annually), Motorcycle (payroll, subject to review), Pay By The Day (iParq, single-day). Valid hours 6:00 AM-12:00 Midnight. Valid lots: Broadway Garage, 13th and E Cherry St Lot, Redhawk Complex Lot (14th & Jefferson), Library Lot (10th & Columbia), Murphy Garage (11th & James Way), Visitor Lot (12th & Marion) only after 3pm and all day weekends.
