# Parking Regulations

**Section:** Transportation & Parking
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > TPS > Parking Regulations

## Content

Seattle University parking rules established for community access. Rain does not alter provisions. University not responsible for theft, damage, or vandalism. TPS and DPS authorized to enforce. SPEED LIMIT: 5 mph max on campus roadways, lots, malls. PEDESTRIANS have right-of-way. OVERNIGHT PARKING of occupied vehicles NOT permitted (includes motor homes, campers). FIRST-YEAR UNDERGRADUATE RESIDENT POLICY: First-year resident students NOT permitted to bring cars during first year (fall-spring). Non-traditional first-years (21+) and off-campus first-years exempt. Pre-college/AP credits don't change first-year status. Waivers available for: Off-campus employment (min 20 hrs, signed/notarized letter required), Medical (licensed physician letter), Extenuating Family Circumstances, University Obligation (athletics, band, internship). Email parkingservices@seattleu.edu. PERMITS: Not transferable except between vehicles registered to same individual. License plates must be visible and unobstructed. CARPOOL: Two or more SU community members may apply. Must arrive/depart as group. Supplemental permits available 1 day/week. IMPOUNDMENT: After two unresolved citations, vehicle may be impounded on third violation. Also for ADA violations, tow-away zones, obstructing traffic, vehicles left 72+ hours unmoved, fraudulent permits. PERMITS NON-REFUNDABLE.
