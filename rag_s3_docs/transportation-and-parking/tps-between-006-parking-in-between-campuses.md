# Parking in Between Campuses

**Section:** Transportation & Parking
**Breadcrumb:** Campus Services > TPS > Parking Between Campuses
**Source URL:** https://redhawks.sharepoint.com/sites/Intranet-CampusServices/SitePages/Parking-in-Between-Campuses.aspx
**Tags:** parking, SLU, Cornish, First Hill, commute

## Content

SLU students with lottery parking: receive monthly invoice; permit valid at First Hill but must register vehicle in FH parking permit system using SU SSO. Students wanting to park at FH: FH fees apply; submit request via Redhawk Hub service portal. Permits include 24/7 Resident Semester, Monthly Resident, Monthly Commuter, Pay-by-Day. Broadway Garage requires First Hill ID Card. Cornish ORCA card works on First Hill Streetcar. Faculty/Staff with Cornish lottery: parking fee deducted through payroll; must still register vehicle through iParq. ORCA Flex permits NOT valid at SLU Campus. For events at SLU: contact slucampusservices@seattleu.edu.
