# Parking FAQ

## Content

Q: When can I buy my permit for next term? A: Permits usually available 1-2 weeks before scheduled start of academic term. Q: If no permits available in iParq, do I still need one? A: Yes, valid permit required all days at all times. Q: If permits are on sale but unavailable in iParq, what do I do? A: Contact Parking Services 206-296-5992 or ParkingServices@seattleu.edu with your name, SU ID, and permit type. Q: Will the University run out of permits? A: No. Q: Citation appeal—can I call/email? A: No, citations managed by Impark. See Citations page. Q: Parking Hold on SU account? A: Prevents diploma/transcripts until balance paid; does NOT prevent class registration.
