## Content

STUDENT PARKING: Permits required all hours, all days. Fall Semester 2025 valid August 18-December 18, 2025. Fall Quarter 2025 valid September 16-December 23, 2025. Carpool participants and 1st-Year On-Campus residents need to submit applications and be approved. On-campus resident permits only for SU students in SU Housing. 1st-Year On-Campus Residents cannot purchase permit unless approved for policy waiver. All students may purchase Pay-By-The-Day permits ($13/day, 6 AM-11:59 PM). Valid parking locations for student permits: Broadway Garage, Murphy Garage, 13th and E. Cherry lot, 14th and E. Jefferson lot, Chardin lot, Library West Lot. Carpool may also use Pigott Visitor Lot before 3pm if 2+ members present.
