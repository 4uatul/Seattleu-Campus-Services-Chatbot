## Content

At Seattle University, Transportation & Parking Services (TPS) helps students, faculty, staff, and guests travel to and from campus. The shuttle service between First Hill and South Lake Union campuses is currently paused as of 11/14/2025. A valid permit is always required when parking on campus, even on weekends, holidays, and breaks. Parking options include daily, monthly, or term permits (via iParq), ORCA Flex permits, PayByPhone sessions, Ventech machine permits (Murphy & Pigott lots), and Guest passes through SU departments. Citation amount: $75.00; penalty totals $93.00 seven days after issued if not paid. Towing may occur upon receipt of third unpaid citation. Beginning summer 2025, Seattle University partners with Impark for parking patrols. Contact: Pavilion 010, Phone 206-296-5992, Email ParkingServices@seattleu.edu. Hours: Mon-Thurs 8:30 AM-4:00 PM, Friday 8:30 AM-2:00 PM, closed weekends and holidays.
